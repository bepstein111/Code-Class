Alex Winzenread                    

twitter heart source: https://vimeo.com/207126117 

first person essay: https://www.sosolimited.com/work/twitter-heart/ 

Sosolimited was approached by Twitter to create a physical sculpture and web tie-in to commemorate the platform rebranding its Favorites as Likes. Likes are s a reflection of the liveliness of conversations happening on Twitter, the team was able to develop a sculpture that reflects the vast amount of communication taking place every minute on Twitter. After framing their analysis of the Twitter experience though tiny interactions like Liking, the team was able to create software to analyze likes and other activity in real-time and cause the heart to pulse in time with the conversation on Twitter. A single like can be personal and exciting, but en masse, they constitute the very lifeblood of the Twitter platform.
